## Getting Started

### Creating Your Account

1. **Visit StartupSniff**: Navigate to the StartupSniff homepage
2. **Sign Up**: Click the "Get Started" or "Sign Up" button
3. **Enter Your Details**: Provide your email address and create a password
4. **Verify Email**: Check your inbox for a verification email and click the confirmation link
5. **Complete Profile**: Add your full name and other optional details

### First Login

After signing in, you'll land on your personalized dashboard. Take a moment to:

- Review your **current plan** (Free, Founder, or Growth)
- Check your **monthly usage limits** (messages and ideas)
- Explore the **Quick Actions** to jump into key features

## Core Features

### Generate Ideas

Transform Reddit pain points into validated startup concepts.

**How to Use:**

1. **Navigate**: Click "Generate Ideas" in the sidebar or Quick Actions
2. **Review Prompts**: The AI will suggest focus areas based on trending Reddit topics
3. **Generate**: Click "Generate Idea" to create a new startup concept
4. **Review Results**: Each idea includes:
   - **Problem Statement**: The pain point being addressed
   - **Target Market**: Who would use this solution
   - **Solution Overview**: How the idea solves the problem
   - **Viability Score**: AI-powered assessment (0-100)
   - **Market Size Estimate**: Potential market opportunity
   - **Key Features**: Core product functionality
5. **Save or Favorite**: Add promising ideas to your collection

**Pro Tips:**
- Generate ideas when you see interesting Reddit discussions
- Use viability scores as a starting point, not the final decision
- Save multiple ideas to compare and contrast later

---

### Browse Opportunities

Discover real problems people are discussing on Reddit right now.

**How to Use:**

1. **Navigate**: Click "Opportunities" in the Market Intelligence section
2. **Filter Results**: Use the powerful filter controls:
   - **Min Score**: Set minimum viability score (0-100)
   - **Subreddit**: Filter by specific communities
   - **Trend Status**: Focus on rising, hot, or new trends
   - **Sort By**: Order by score, engagement, or recency
3. **Review Cards**: Each opportunity shows:
   - **Viability Score Badge**: Color-coded rating (green = high, yellow = medium, red = low)
   - **Subreddit Tag**: Where the discussion is happening
   - **Engagement Metrics**: Upvotes and comments
   - **Trend Indicator**: Rising, hot, or new
   - **AI Analysis Badge**: Whether AI has analyzed this opportunity
4. **View Details**: Click "View Details" for in-depth analysis
5. **Take Action**: Generate an idea or start a conversation

**Understanding Viability Scores:**
- **80-100**: Excellent opportunity with clear market demand
- **60-79**: Good opportunity worth exploring
- **40-59**: Moderate opportunity, requires validation
- **0-39**: Low viability, significant challenges

### My Conversations

Connect directly with potential customers on Reddit (Pro feature).

**How to Use:**

1. **Navigate**: Click "My Conversations" in Market Intelligence
2. **Connect Reddit**: First-time setup:
   - Click "Connect Reddit Account"
   - Authorize StartupSniff to access your Reddit account
   - Grant permissions for messaging
3. **Find Contacts**: The AI identifies relevant users from opportunity threads
4. **Generate Messages**: For each contact:
   - Review AI-generated message variants (4 options)
   - Customize the message if needed
   - Preview before sending
5. **Send & Track**: Monitor:
   - **Messages Sent**: Total outreach count
   - **Response Rate**: Percentage of replies received
   - **Engagement Score**: Quality of conversations
6. **Follow Up**: Continue conversations directly on Reddit

**Best Practices:**
- Personalize AI-generated messages
- Be genuine and helpful, not salesy
- Follow up within 24 hours of responses
- Respect Reddit community guidelines
- Track which message variants perform best

**Upgrade Required:**
Free plan users get 10 messages per month. Upgrade to Founder ($19/month) or Growth ($49/month) for unlimited messaging.

### Trend Insights

Analyze market trends and emerging opportunities across Reddit.

**How to Use:**

1. **Navigate**: Click "Trend Insights" in Market Intelligence
2. **View Trending Topics**: See what's gaining traction
3. **Analyze Patterns**: Review:
   - **Topic Velocity**: How fast topics are growing
   - **Engagement Trends**: User interaction over time
   - **Sentiment Analysis**: Positive vs. negative discussions
   - **Geographic Distribution**: Where discussions are happening
4. **Identify Gaps**: Find underserved niches
5. **Generate Ideas**: Transform trends into startup concepts

**Insight Types:**
- **Rising Trends**: New topics gaining momentum (catch early)
- **Hot Trends**: Currently popular discussions (high engagement)
- **Declining Trends**: Losing interest (avoid or pivot)
- **Seasonal Patterns**: Recurring opportunities

### Validation Lab

Test and validate your startup ideas with AI-powered market research.

**How to Use:**

1. **Navigate**: Click "Validation Lab" in Market Intelligence
2. **Select Idea**: Choose an idea from your saved collection or enter a new one
3. **Run Validation**: The AI performs:
   - **Market Size Analysis**: TAM, SAM, SOM calculations
   - **Competitive Landscape**: Similar solutions and differentiation
   - **Customer Validation**: Reddit sentiment analysis
   - **Technical Feasibility**: Build complexity assessment
   - **Go-to-Market Strategy**: Distribution channel recommendations
4. **Review Report**: Detailed validation results with:
   - **Overall Score**: 0-100 validation rating
   - **Risk Assessment**: Major challenges and risks
   - **Recommendations**: Next steps to pursue or pivot
5. **Make Decision**: Save, iterate, or archive based on findings

**Validation Criteria:**
- ‚úÖ **Problem-Market Fit**: Does the problem really exist?
- ‚úÖ **Solution Viability**: Can this be built?
- ‚úÖ **Market Opportunity**: Is the market big enough?
- ‚úÖ **Competition**: Can you differentiate?

## Tips & Best Practices

### For Idea Discovery

1. **Browse Daily**: New opportunities appear every day
2. **Filter Wisely**: Start with high-viability scores (80+)
3. **Read Comments**: The gold is often in Reddit comment threads
4. **Track Trends**: Save interesting trends even if not pursuing yet
5. **Combine Insights**: Look for patterns across multiple subreddits

### For Customer Conversations

1. **Be Authentic**: Reddit users value genuine interactions
2. **Provide Value First**: Help before pitching
3. **Respect Community Rules**: Each subreddit has guidelines
4. **Test Message Variants**: Use A/B testing to improve response rates
5. **Follow Up**: Persistence (polite) pays off

### For Validation

1. **Validate Early**: Don't fall in love with ideas too quickly
2. **Seek Disconfirming Evidence**: Look for reasons it won't work
3. **Talk to Real Users**: Supplement AI validation with interviews
4. **Iterate Quickly**: Test, learn, adapt
5. **Set Clear Criteria**: Define what "validated" means for you

### For Productivity

1. **Set Weekly Goals**: e.g., "Review 10 opportunities this week"
2. **Use Favorites**: Mark your top ideas for quick access
3. **Export Regularly**: Backup your research
4. **Review Monthly**: Look at your progress and patterns
5. **Upgrade When Ready**: Don't let limits slow your momentum

## FAQ

### General

**Q: How often are new opportunities added?**
A: Our cron job fetches fresh Reddit posts every 4 hours, analyzing 15+ subreddits. You'll see new opportunities daily.

**Q: How accurate is the AI viability scoring?**
A: Our AI combines GPT-4o analysis with Reddit engagement metrics for 87%+ accuracy. Always supplement with your own research.

**Q: Can I suggest subreddits to monitor?**
A: Yes! Contact support with subreddit suggestions. We prioritize based on user requests.

**Q: Is my data private?**
A: Absolutely. We never share your ideas or conversations. See our Privacy Policy for details.

### Account & Billing

**Q: Can I change plans mid-month?**
A: Yes! Upgrades are instant with pro-rated billing. Downgrades take effect at the next billing cycle.

**Q: What happens when I hit my monthly limit?**
A: You'll receive an email notification. You can either upgrade or wait for the monthly reset.

**Q: Do unused messages roll over?**
A: No, limits reset monthly on your billing date. Pro plans have unlimited usage.

**Q: Can I get a refund?**
A: We offer 7-day money-back guarantee, no questions asked. Contact support.

### Features

**Q: Why do I need to connect my Reddit account?**
A: For the "My Conversations" feature, we need permission to send messages on your behalf. Your credentials are never stored.

**Q: Can I export my data?**
A: Founder and Growth plans can export ideas as CSV or JSON. Free plan users can copy/paste.

**Q: Do you support other platforms besides Reddit?**
A: Currently Reddit-focused. Growth plan includes experimental multi-platform trends (Twitter, Indie Hackers).

**Q: Can I collaborate with my team?**
A: Team features are coming in Q2 2026. Stay tuned!

### Technical

**Q: Which browsers are supported?**
A: Chrome, Firefox, Safari, Edge (latest 2 versions). Mobile browsers supported.

**Q: Is there a mobile app?**
A: Not yet, but our web app is fully mobile-responsive. Native app planned for 2026.

**Q: Can I use the API?**
A: API access is available for Growth plan users. Documentation at `/docs/api`.

**Q: What about rate limits?**
A: API rate limits: 100 req/hour (Free), 500 req/hour (Founder), 2000 req/hour (Growth).
